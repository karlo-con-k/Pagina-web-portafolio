Reconocimiento estadístico
de patrone

This work consisted of conducting an exploration of poverty indicators at municipal level using data from 2020 and data from 2015 as a comparison. Throughout the work, tools such as T-SNE, SOM, hierarchical clustering, and others were used.

I started with data cleaning, identifying the municipalities with the least amount of data loss and the data types with the least amount of missing information. Therefore, I decided to focus on them. I also noticed that I could work with the data as a time series (visualization of the data as a time series).



We project our data onto the two principal components (PCA) to obtain an initial visualization of the data.





After analyzing the results, we will apply ISOMAP to our data to obtain another perspective on them. When we do this with n = 2, we get the following image.





Continuing with our data analysis, we applied T-SNE to our data with 2 components to start working on the separation our data into sets.




Continuing with our analysis, we applied Hierarchical Clustering to obtain a better separation of the data sets we have.




The complete work is available in the following PDF , where each of the results obtained by the models used is interpreted in greater depth, as well as the application of other tools and models for data analysis to gain a better understanding of our data.
