This work/project was based on the publication 'Residual Learning of Deep CNN for Image Denoising'.I read the article, implemented and designed a neural network based on it, and compared it with the original network. I obtained better results with my network.

The article in question presents a neural network (which we will call 'network 1') that we will use in our study. This neural network removes noise from images.





After researching each layer of the model presented in the article and reading some related papers. I found a study titled Searching for Activation Functions , which explains that by changing the activation layers to Swish layers, the behavior of the model becomes more stable and has better convergence. Also, variants of the network 1 were analyzed, such as the removal of normalization layers or the reduction of the number of layers. It was known in advance that these tests would not produce good results, but they were carried out mainly to highlight the importance of the elements that were removed.
