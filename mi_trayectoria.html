My career

I started my academic journey in high school. Where I chose to study mathematics in the Department of Mathematics at the University of Guanajuato, or DEMAT.

I initially faced challenges in my academic journey, struggling to understand class topics and failing despite putting in considerable effort. However, I made a determined decision to succeed. I adopted a rigorous routine, attending classes, studying extensively, and relentlessly working on improving my skills. This dedication led to passing my courses and a continuous pursuit of academic challenges, including master's level courses and summer schools.

I decided to focus on Computer Science throughout my career. I took around 50% of the courses for the Master's degree at CIMAT while I was in the degree. Even though it would have been more comfortable for me to pursue a Master's degree in Computer Science, I decided to step out of my comfort zone and look for a job in that field.
